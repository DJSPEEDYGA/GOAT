Signal modes (top-left panel in every game):
• Simulated EEG — realistic trainer dynamics, play instantly with no hardware.
• Manual — drive the score with a slider (demo / instructor mode).
• Live Device (LSL bridge) — connects to ws://localhost:8765, a relay that forwards Lab Streaming Layer samples from a Muse or OpenBCI headset as {"calm":0–100,"focus":0–100}. Same games, real brainwaves — no code changes needed.
