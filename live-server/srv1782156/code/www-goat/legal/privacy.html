Privacy Policy

How GOAT Royalty Store collects, uses, stores, shares, retains, and deletes your information.

Effective 2026-06-30 · Last updated 2026-06-30

Operator: Life Imitates Art Inc
Service: GOAT Royalty Store (goatroyalty.app)
Contact / data requests: support@goatroyalty.app

1. Who we are

GOAT Royalty Store ("we", "us") is operated by Life Imitates Art Inc. This policy applies to goatroyalty.app and the GOAT Royalty Store application.

2. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account and contact details (e.g. name, email) when you register, request access, or contact us.
  • Content you submit (messages, form submissions, profile information).

Information collected automatically

  • Device and usage data: IP address, browser/user-agent, pages viewed, and timestamps, used for security, abuse prevention, and reliability.
  • Cookies and similar local storage — see our Cookie Policy.

3. How we use information

  • To provide, operate, secure, and improve the service you requested.
  • To communicate with you about your account or requests.
  • To prevent fraud and abuse and to comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal bases (GDPR/where applicable)

We process data to perform our contract with you, for legitimate interests (security, improvement), to comply with legal obligations, and with your consent where required (e.g. optional integrations and non-essential cookies).

5. Connected third-party platforms & developer APIs

When you choose to connect a third-party account, or when we use a platform's developer API to provide a feature you requested, we access only the data needed for that feature. We use this data in accordance with each platform's developer terms and policies, we do not sell it, and we do not use it for unrelated purposes.

Your control

  • You can disconnect any linked platform at any time from your account settings or by emailing us.
  • Disconnecting revokes our future access; we then delete or de-identify the associated data per the retention rules below, except records we must keep by law.
  • Where a platform (e.g. TikTok, Spotify) provides its own data-revocation controls, you may also revoke our access directly through that platform.

6. How we share information

We do not sell personal data. We share it only with: (a) service providers who process data on our behalf under contract (e.g. hosting); (b) the platforms you explicitly connect, as needed for that integration; and (c) authorities where required by law.

7. Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law, then delete or de-identify it. Security logs are kept for a limited period; legally required records (e.g. tax, dispute, safety) are kept for their mandated periods.

8. Your rights & data deletion

  • You may request access, correction, export (portability), or deletion of your personal data, and may object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Deletion path: email support@goatroyalty.app with the subject "Data Deletion" (include the account/handle and any connected platform). We verify and action verified requests within 30 days, except records we must retain by law.
  • If you connected a third-party platform, deleting your data here also ends our use of that platform's data for your account.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures (encryption in transit, access controls, audit logging). No method is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data and notify you of material breaches where required.

10. Children

GOAT Royalty Store is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 where applicable). We do not knowingly collect their data and will delete it if discovered.

11. International transfers

We may process data in countries other than yours. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g. standard contractual clauses) for cross-border transfers.

12. Changes

We will update this policy as the service evolves and post the new effective date here. Material changes will be highlighted in-product where feasible.

13. Contact

Questions or requests: support@goatroyalty.app.
