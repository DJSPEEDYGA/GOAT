# AGENT-007 Icon Art Lab Brief

Classification: owner-local sanitized brief

## Mission

AGENT-007 and the GOAT crew now have a dedicated Icon Art Lab lane for original visual production. The lane covers icons, app symbols, logos, cover art, thumbnails, title cards, stickers, visual prompts, 3D concept boards, UI skins, and client-safe visual packages.

## What AGENT-007 Should Build

- Original icon packs for GOAT, AGENT-007, Money Penny, Lexi, Ms. Vanessa, Ms. Nexus, Sir Codex, Accord/The Terminal, tools, models, clips, cameras, studios, vaults, payments, catalog scanning, and fingerprinting.
- Cover art and thumbnail briefs for songs, albums, sync pitches, podcasts, videos, promo clips, and client work.
- Prompt packs for owner-approved image generators, including subject, style, lighting, camera language, palette, aspect ratio, negative prompt, and export target.
- Brand kits with palette, typography notes, logo lockups, avatar frames, social templates, and client handoff checklists.
- 3D concept boards with scene layout, camera blocking, material ideas, render passes, and Thor/GPU migration notes.

## Quality Standard

- Clear silhouette at small sizes.
- Consistent grid, stroke, corner radius, contrast, spacing, and export sizes.
- Every asset has source, owner/license status, hash, version, intended use, and public/client-safe status.
- Every live replacement has preview, rollback, and fresh owner approval.
- Every client package separates source/private files from approved public exports.

## Crew Routing

- AGENT-007 coordinates the request and approval gate.
- Money Penny connects visuals to releases, DSPs, campaigns, invoices, client packages, and monetization.
- Lexi builds manifests, SVG helpers, resizing/export scripts, prompt forms, and app integration.
- Ms. Vanessa verifies provenance, rights-risk, hashes, consent, and chain-of-custody.
- Ms. Nexus routes visuals to platforms, partners, creators, clients, and rollout testing.
- Sir Codex maintains SOPs, QA sheets, naming rules, and implementation plans.

## Safety Rules

- Do not copy protected icons, logos, brand trade dress, album art, game art, app UI assets, paid assets, or proprietary prompt packs.
- Do not imitate a private person's likeness without consent.
- Do not publish or upload anything containing private Drive links, vault phrases, API keys, payment info, client secrets, or unreleased file names.
- Use owner-created, licensed, open-license, public-domain, or generated assets that pass rights review.

## First Useful Outputs

1. Icon pack manifest.
2. SVG sprite starter pack.
3. Album/thumbnail art brief.
4. Prompt pack.
5. Export size matrix.
6. Rights/provenance sheet.
7. Preview and rollback plan.
